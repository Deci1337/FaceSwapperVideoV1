## Core Libraries

### InsightFace
- **Author:** DeepInsight Team
- **Repository:** https://github.com/deepinsight/insightface
- **License:** MIT License
- **Usage:** Face detection, recognition, and face swapping (inswapper_128 model)

### GFPGAN
- **Authors:** Xintao Wang, Yu Li, Honglun Zhang, Ying Shan
- **Repository:** https://github.com/TencentARC/GFPGAN
- **Paper:** "GFP-GAN: Towards Real-World Blind Face Restoration with Generative Facial Prior" (CVPR 2021)
- **License:** Apache License 2.0
- **Usage:** Face enhancement and restoration

### MediaPipe
- **Author:** Google Research
- **Repository:** https://github.com/google/mediapipe
- **License:** Apache License 2.0
- **Usage:** Hand skeleton detection, face mesh, selfie segmentation

### OpenCV
- **Author:** OpenCV Team
- **Repository:** https://github.com/opencv/opencv
- **License:** Apache License 2.0
- **Usage:** Video/image processing, color correction
